Our values, our history


Nitrux S.A.® aims to create and build something better every time. We are doing things that will put us as a next generation global company. Fresher design approaches, newer platforms, newer technologies to provide the fastest computing experience for our users. Our mission is to become one of the world’s greatest technology companies.


At Nitrux® we balance all that we do and that works for us as an effective business model. This mindset encompasses every aspect of Nitrux®'s business. Our values as a company define who we are and how we work. of Open Source.


We want to succeed in bringing our customers satisfaction when they use our products, offering innovative computing solutions. We also believe that the knowledge is for all, and for that we want to make sure our customers realize the potential.


In April 2012 Nitrux® came about as the result of the decision to pursue a new idea in UI design. Initially a hobby project it became increasingly complex as more and more ideas were discussed and later on it was decided not to focus solely on UI design but on improving the overall state of operating systems and computers in general.


Starting in March 2013 plans to make the the next generation desktop operating system came into place, with a team from all over the world, and many more showing interest in the new design approach that was going to take place, the company now prepares to become a global brand.


While those are our dreams and our goals, we know of the roller-coaster that awaits us, we're not working on something that you ll hear us say "it'll right everything that's wrong in our tech-enabled world", but if we can be that alternative and help improve what's right we will have succeeded.


We don't want to be just another company, we want to change things, we want to support those who spend heir free time doing wonderful stuff, because we believe that their effort should be rewarded, and because we believe that we need more of these people.
