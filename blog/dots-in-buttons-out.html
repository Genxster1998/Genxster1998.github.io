Blog: Dots in, buttons out

Posted on January 05, 2014 by Uri Herrera

The Nitrux® icon theme has come a long way since its release dated April 1, 2012, and throughout the long journey some variations of the original theme were available. One of them is NITRUX-Buttons, this variant has been accompanying the main theme for well over 1.5 years at one point taking over the focus of Nitrux® itself, but with the advent of iNX a theme meant to be more of a test-bed for a new design approach which was later incorporated into Nitrux® and then Compass who's minimalism brought back the project to it's roots, NITRUX-Buttons is now more of a legacy part constituting the whole theme, and as such to keep improving our artwork it's time to let it go.

NITRUX-Buttons will no longer be updated or supported in future Nitrux® releases nor it will be available through official channels. This is because new projects need the work and effort that was put into NITRUX-Buttons, Dots our new theme is an all new theme altogether while NITRUX-Buttons is more of a simple variation of the original theme to provide a more mobile freindly icon theme however Compass has provided that so there's no need for NITRUX-Buttons anymore however if you're a fan of it and you're good with Inkscape or a compatible SVG editor, you can join Nitrux® S.A as a volunteer and maintain the theme, if you're interested you can always contact us through Facebook, Twitter, Google+ or our mail (links at the bottom).
