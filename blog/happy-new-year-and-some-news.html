Blog: Happy new year and some news

Posted on December 31, 2013 by Uri Herrera

So, first of all, happy new year! everyone and greetings from Mexico, now on to the blog. So we've heard the feedback about our new site, our new visual branding and our new direction specially as an OEM and we've got some cool news. We'll add a new icon theme to our ever growing collection of themes, along with the bundles for Compass. The Basic bundle for Compass will feature the Compass-mono-dark icons to be used on dark themes also it'll contain 4 folder addons for Compass. The Super bundle for Compass will include the contents of the Basic bundle + Dots + 3 more folder variations. The Mega bundle will be available later on as it will include the Android version of Compass and Dots.

What is Dots anyways?, well it's our new icon theme, a round, simple and minimal icon theme that replaces the app icons of Compass. Now some more news, we've heard your feedback regarding the NXQ®, we appreciate your input on it as it'll help us improve it. We'd like to clear up some things about it, first of all the shipping price, as you know (or not) we're located in Mexico (crazy ain't it?), we being a company are subject to company taxes and those are higher than your average citizen taxes, now it gets more interesting as a company that intends to have international presence we to export our devices to the outside world, now that's the important thing to remember, we export goods/electronics and export taxes are high, we might be residing in a 3rd world country but our taxes are certainly 1st class. Suffice to say that we pay more per Mbit than every other country and we have as much as 10 times slower internet than everyone else. Anyways, unfortunately that makes the export shipping price higher than what you might see from other places. And as such it's something we can't control. It is what it is.

Second, the price, that we can do something about and we have, we've working hard to be able to reduce the price of the device and we have succeded in being able to reduce it as much as 100 USD, much less than what it was before. The new pricing for the NXQ® models will be 200 USD for the 8 GB version, 225 USD for the 16GB version and 275 USD for the 64GB version, we hope that these new prices will be more compelling to our fans and customers. We also will be adding as a feature to the site the ability to select what materials the body of the NXQ® is made of, ranging from plastic to wood and glass, this is to be expected mid-way through 2014. On that tune the design of the QtBox® will have some changes, mostly aesthethic but it will retain it's current design. So back at work it is.
