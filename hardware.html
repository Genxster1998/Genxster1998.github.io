All the power, none of the space

The NXQ® is the next step in desktop PC form factors, it's a small and portable device. With it you can access the very latest technolgy and Open Source® applications. The sleek design and performance specs combined with the huge variety of software available for it make the NXQ® the perfect replacement for your PC.

The NXQ® runs on our preferred Linux platform, the KDE Software Compilation. KDE is one of the most popular desktop environments in Linux, it's incredibly packed with features and performance. With the NXQ® you can carry your own desktop with you, meetings, presentations all are simplified by just hooking up the device to an HDMI capable screen, plug-in your wireless accessories and you'll be able to access your files just like you would at home.

Just as it is a work PC it is a teaching tool, available for the NXQ® are the most popular IDEs and SDKs to be used as a learning tool, also in the developer section you'll find documentation and resources to further increase the value of the device as a way to teach anyone about the inner workings of computers and programming.

Not doing work?, you can also use your NXQ® to enjoy your media files using Xine. Powered by a Mali 400 MP4 graphics chip capable of hardware video decoding at HD, the NXQ® provides the most sensible entertainment hub. Using the wireless drives available at the Nitrux Store® you'll be able to stream your files to the NXQ® without a hassle.

In our stance to promote and someday fund open projects we also want to invite schools and businesses to drop their ages old, slow, unresponsive machines loaded with pirated Windows® copies and change them for new, open, fast machines with a legally distributed OS that will teach and show the benefits from Open Source®. It will showcase the work of people from all around the globe that will spark interest in something that is not much of a concern in 3rd world countries like ours, which is technology.
